- Graph files support both .gr and .edges format
- proof modes with -p can now select between no proofs, drat- proofs and ER proofs
- distance rerolls (previously -m 1) are now selected by -m 6
- changes to terminal texts and debugging tools (requires manual editing .cpp file)
